Ascension Cathedral
Working hours: daily from 7:00 to 18:00
Travel time from the international airport: 23
minutes
Learn more
  • The Ascension Cathedral is an Orthodox church in Alma—Ata, on the territory of the park named after 28 Panfilov Guards, one of the two cathedrals of the Astana and Almaty dioceses of the Russian Orthodox Church.

    It was built in the Pushkin Park of the city of Verniy, Semirechensk region in 1904-1907 as the cathedral of the Turkestan diocese.

    With a height of 41.4 (up to the bell tower's cross ball) or 54 m in general, it is one of the tallest wooden churches in the world; an example of earthquake-resistant structures. It is included in the list of historical and cultural monuments of Kazakhstan of republican significance.

Central Park
culture and recreation
Working hours: all year round
Travel time from the international airport: no data available
More detailed
  • The Central Park of Culture and Recreation is located in the Medeu district of Almaty. The park was founded in 1856 by the horticulturist G.Krishtopenko and was called the "State Garden". The park was a place for walks and officers of the Vernensky garrison.

    In 1934, the park was reconstructed, recreation centers for workers of Alma-Ata were placed on the banks of the deepened reservoir, and attractions were equipped.

    On the territory of the park there are children's attractions, a children's railway, winter and summer water parks, sports grounds, boat and catamaran rental stations, various cafes, a dinopark.

Medeu
Working hours: all year round
Travel time from the international airport: no data available
More detailed
  • Medeu Ice rink, located at an altitude of 1691.2 meters above sea level. It was erected in 1972. The ice surface occupies 10.5 thousand sq.m., which makes it possible to hold competitions in speed skating, hockey and figure skating.

    The mild climate in the gorge, the optimal level of solar radiation, low atmospheric pressure, weak wind and clear glacial water – all this provided the alpine skating rink with wide popularity. More than 120 world records have been set here by famous athletes.
